About Us

A trusted local commercial cleaning company built on long-term relationships.

Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc. has provided dependable commercial cleaning services throughout Sarnia-Lambton and surrounding communities since 2015, supporting businesses, public facilities, community organizations, property managers, recreational facilities, and select residential clients.

Who We Are

Relationship-driven cleaning services since 2015.

Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc. is a proudly Canadian-owned and operated cleaning company serving Sarnia, Lambton County, and surrounding communities.

The company has grown through referrals, repeat business, long-term client relationships, responsive communication, and dependable service.

We believe professional cleaning is about more than maintaining a building. It's about creating environments where people can work, serve, learn, and gather with confidence.

Every client relationship begins with a free, no-obligation consultation. We take the time to understand your facility or property, operational requirements, priorities, and expectations before recommending a customized cleaning program.

Mission

To provide reliable, professional cleaning services that help businesses, organizations, workplaces, public facilities, managed properties, and homeowners maintain clean, welcoming, functional, and well-cared-for environments.

Vision

To be a trusted cleaning partner for commercial clients, community organizations, public facilities, property managers, real estate professionals, and residential customers by delivering dependable service built on communication, accountability, and respect.

Cleaning Philosophy

Clean spaces support the people who use them.

Why Clean Spaces Matter

Cleaning Supports Function

Clean spaces help people work, meet, gather, learn, serve their communities, and perform daily activities comfortably and effectively.

Cleaning Supports Comfort

Clean environments contribute to a more welcoming and pleasant experience for employees, visitors, customers, residents, and community members.

Healthier Environments

Regular cleaning and sanitation help manage the buildup of dust, dirt, allergens, microorganisms, and everyday contaminants.

Cleaning Protects Investments

Proper cleaning helps maintain floors, furniture, fixtures, glass, washrooms, common areas, and shared spaces.

Consistent Attention to Detail

We believe great cleaning is the result of consistent attention to detail. Every visit is approached with care, following structured cleaning methods that help deliver dependable, high-quality results over time.

Use the right product for the right job.

We select janitorial products and cleaning methods based on the surface, environment, occupancy, desired outcome, safety considerations, and client preferences.

Every Facility Is Different

Every facility has unique needs. A municipal office, church, commercial building, community organization, or residential property each requires different cleaning methods, products, schedules, and priorities. We adapt our approach to the environment rather than relying on a one-size-fits-all process.

Consistency Matters

Great cleaning is built on consistency. We follow structured cleaning methods designed to deliver dependable, high-quality results visit after visit, while remaining flexible to the specific needs of each client and facility.
