Deep Cleaning

Deep Cleaning Services

Deep cleaning is a more thorough, periodic service that goes beyond routine janitorial maintenance, addressing floors, carpets, and areas that don't get fully covered during regular visits. It's commonly used to reset a space before starting a new recurring program, and then on an ongoing periodic basis afterward to maintain a higher standard over time.

Thorough periodic deep cleaning for offices, public facilities, and organizations throughout Sarnia-Lambton.

Typical Facilities

Where this service is commonly provided.

Deep cleaning is used across most facility types, typically as a periodic complement to routine janitorial service.

  • Offices transitioning to a new cleaning provider
  • Public & community facilities
  • Retail & commercial spaces
  • Property management common areas
  • High-traffic recreational facilities
  • Spaces due for a seasonal reset

Who This Service Is For

For facilities starting fresh or maintaining a higher standard over time.

Deep cleaning is often requested by facilities beginning a new cleaning program, or by existing clients on a periodic basis to maintain results between routine visits.

Our Approach

A deep clean sets the standard routine service maintains.

We often recommend an initial deep cleaning when a client switches providers, so routine service starts from a consistent, known baseline rather than an unknown one.

After that, most facilities don't need deep cleaning on every visit. It's typically scheduled periodically alongside routine janitorial maintenance.

Scheduling & Service Frequency

Frequency is based on how your facility is actually used.

Deep cleaning frequency depends on facility traffic and how well routine maintenance is keeping pace between visits.

Frequency Examples
Initial Deep Clean Common when starting a new recurring cleaning program.
Quarterly A common periodic interval for many facility types.
Semi-Annual Suited to lower-traffic spaces maintained well by routine service.
Seasonal Timed around seasonal conditions or facility needs.
As-Needed Scheduled in response to a specific facility concern.
Combined with Routine Service Layered alongside your existing janitorial schedule.

These are examples only. Your actual cleaning frequency is confirmed during a site assessment, not assumed in advance.
