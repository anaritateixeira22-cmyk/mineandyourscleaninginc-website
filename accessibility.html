Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to accessible communication.

Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc. aims to provide clear, usable, and accessible information for website visitors, clients, applicants, and community members.

Last updated: July 2026

Our Commitment

Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc. is committed to providing information and communication in a way that respects accessibility, dignity, and usability.

Website Accessibility

We aim to make this website reasonably accessible and easy to use, including through clear navigation, readable text, structured headings, labelled forms, and mobile-friendly layouts.

Accessible Communication

If you require information in another format, need assistance using our website, or prefer an alternate communication method, please contact us and we will do our best to respond in a practical and reasonable way.

Employment Applications

Applicants who need assistance with the employment application process may contact us directly. We will work with applicants where reasonable to support communication and application accessibility.

Service Inquiries

Clients or prospective clients who need assistance submitting a service inquiry may contact us by phone, text message, voicemail, or email.

Ongoing Improvements

We may update this website over time to improve accessibility, usability, readability, and compatibility with different devices and assistive technologies.

Website & Operational Development

This website forms part of Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc.'s ongoing commitment to accessibility, operational excellence, and continuous improvement.

Website architecture, operational systems, business process development, documentation, accessibility implementation, and digital workflow design were developed by Rita Teixeira in support of the company's long-term growth and service delivery.

Feedback

We welcome feedback about accessibility barriers on this website or in our communication process.
