How Partnerships Work

Built around direct accountability.

Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc. performs the services we provide using our own team whenever possible. Our clients work directly with us, and we remain responsible for the quality, communication, and management of every cleaning program we deliver.

This page is intended as an invitation for businesses interested in exploring future partnership, referral, or subcontracting opportunities. It should not be interpreted as a list of our current partners or subcontractors.

Should a situation ever require collaboration with another organization, it would only occur following careful evaluation and, where applicable, appropriate communication, documentation, and client approval.

Partnership Standards

Listing a category does not imply an existing partnership.

Potential partnerships are evaluated based on factors including:

  • Experience and reputation
  • Reliability and professionalism
  • Appropriate insurance coverage for their service and sector
  • Health and safety practices
  • Applicable certifications and licensing
  • Quality standards
  • Operational compatibility
  • Commitment to client service

Our goal is to build long-term relationships with organizations that share our commitment to quality, professionalism, accountability, safety, and exceptional client care.
