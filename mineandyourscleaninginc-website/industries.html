Industries We Serve

Cleaning programs built for the way different facilities operate.

Mine And Yours Cleaning Inc. works with a range of commercial, public-sector, and community organizations throughout Sarnia & Lambton County. Every industry below has different traffic patterns, access requirements, and priorities, and our commercial cleaning, janitorial, and deep cleaning services are scoped to fit each one. Selected residential cleaning remains available on a secondary basis.
