Careers

Join a team that takes pride in doing the job right.

At Mine And Yours Cleaning, we're looking for dependable people who take pride in their work, communicate well, and respect the clients and facilities we serve.

What We Look For

Reliable people who care about doing the job properly.

Many of our clients trust us with access to workplaces, public facilities, community spaces, and private properties. That's why reliability, professionalism, communication, and attention to detail are just as important as cleaning skills.

What We Look For
Reliable attendance and communication Consistent scheduling clients can count on
Respect for client privacy and property Discretion in every space we work in
Ability to follow instructions and checklists Consistent results across every visit
Attention to detail The small things that make a space feel clean
Reliable transportation Ability to travel to assigned work locations
Background Check Eligibility Applicants must be willing and able to complete a Criminal Record Check as a condition of employment.
