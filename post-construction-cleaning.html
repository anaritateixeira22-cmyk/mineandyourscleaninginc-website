Post-Construction Cleaning

Post-Construction Cleaning Services

Post-construction cleaning prepares a space for occupancy after renovation, build-out, or construction work has finished. It's a one-time, intensive service that clears construction dust, fine particles, and loose debris left behind by trades work, along with normal curbside-level garbage. Larger items and materials that require special disposal, such as drywall offcuts, leftover piping or wiring, siding, old furniture, or doors, are handled separately as part of the renovation project itself, following local disposal requirements. The same applies to any other bulky item or material along those lines, not just the examples listed here. Because fine dust continues to settle after an initial pass, the work is typically done in stages before the space is ready to hand over.

Post-construction cleanup preparing renovated and newly built spaces for occupancy throughout Sarnia-Lambton.

Typical Facilities

Where this service is commonly provided.

Post-construction cleaning applies wherever renovation or construction work has recently taken place.

  • Renovated office spaces
  • New or renovated retail spaces
  • Public & municipal building renovations
  • Property management units after renovation
  • Community facility upgrades
  • Newly built commercial spaces

Who This Service Is For

For spaces ready to transition from construction to occupancy.

Post-construction cleaning is typically used by contractors, property managers, or businesses preparing a renovated or newly built space for use.

Our Approach

A different kind of cleanup, not just a bigger clean.

Construction dust behaves differently than everyday dirt, so post-construction cleaning often uses different equipment and steps than routine or deep cleaning, including HEPA-filtered vacuums and dedicated passes for paint or adhesive residue.

Many clients move directly from post-construction cleaning into a recurring commercial cleaning or janitorial schedule once the space is occupied.

Scheduling & Service Frequency

Frequency is based on how your facility is actually used.

This is generally a one-time or short-term service rather than a recurring one, though the scope may involve more than one visit for larger projects.

Frequency Examples
One-Time Project Cleaning The most common format for post-construction cleanup.
Multi-Visit Projects Larger renovations may require more than one cleaning visit.

These are examples only. Your actual cleaning frequency is confirmed during a site assessment, not assumed in advance.
